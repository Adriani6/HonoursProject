Features

Follow travellers around the world and see what they are up to, where they go and what they see.
Follow Tourism Agences and see their recommended list of attractions and places to see. No more waiting and paying hotel recommended agencies.
Quickly and easily plan your journey on the go!
Communicate with travellers in your area easily! Make plans & meet new people.
Extensive search tools to plan your journey. See what's on your way to your destination or what's already around your radius.
Create your wish list of places you want to see.
If you're a business owner, promoting your business is easier with TravelCards which can attract customers easier with our search tools.
